# dirb CheatSheet

* Basic use
  * `$ dirb http://page.com`
  * `$ dirb http://1.2.3.4`

* Selecting your wordlist
  * `$ dirb http://page.com /path/to/wordlist`

* Selecting your user-agent
  * `$ dirb http://page.com -a "my-user-agent"`

* Saved in a file
  * `$ dirb http://page.com -o output.txt`
